The "Inbox Zero" Principle

A productivity system to keep your inbox empty by taking immediate action on every message.

‚öôÔ∏è Five Actions of Inbox Zero:

  1. Delete: Remove spam and irrelevant messages
  2. Delegate: Forward to the right person
  3. Respond: If under 2 minutes, reply now
  4. Defer: Snooze for later if more time needed
  5. Do: Complete the task, then archive
Example: Each morning, process emails by deleting promos, forwarding inquiries, responding to appointments, snoozing follow-ups, and archiving completed items.
